Random Table Box Generator Thingy

Enter a number in the box. A maaaaagic table will appear, fit to the size of your number.

Each of the cells in the table will hold a random number between 1 and 100.

If the number is a multiple of 3, the cell will have a red background color

If it's not a multiple of 3 but a multiple of 2, the cell will have a blue background color.

Finally, the power of internet maaaaaagic will display the average for all cells.
